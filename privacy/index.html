Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Get Your Deal ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services, including our integration with the Pinterest API.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the site.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact information (e.g., email address) when you reach out to us
  • Any content or feedback you voluntarily submit to us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps)
  • Device information (browser version, operating system)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6)

2.3 Information via Pinterest API

Our application integrates with the Pinterest API to publish affiliate deal pins on our Pinterest boards. Through this integration, we may access:

  • Our own Pinterest account profile data (username, account ID)
  • Our own board names and pin data
  • Pinterest API usage metrics

⚠️ We do not access, collect, or store any Pinterest user data, follower information, or third-party account information. We only use the Pinterest API to post content to our own boards.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve our website and deal-sharing services
  • Publish curated affiliate deal pins to Pinterest via the Pinterest API
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Pinterest API Compliance

Our use of the Pinterest API complies with Pinterest's Developer Guidelines and Pinterest's Terms of Service . Specifically:

  • We only use Pinterest API access tokens for our own authorized account
  • We do not scrape, cache, or redistribute Pinterest user data
  • We do not use the Pinterest API to spam, mislead, or post deceptive content
  • All content posted via the API is original, clearly labelled affiliate content
  • We store API access tokens securely and never expose them publicly
  • We respect Pinterest's rate limits and usage policies

5. Affiliate Links Disclosure

Our website and Pinterest pins contain affiliate links. This means we may earn a commission if you click a link and make a purchase, at no extra cost to you. We only promote brands and products we believe provide genuine value. All affiliate relationships are clearly disclosed.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the website functions correctly. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some parts of the site may not function correctly as a result.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Pinterest – via API calls to post pins (subject to Pinterest's own privacy policy)
  • Service providers – hosting or analytics services that help us operate the website, bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Legal authorities – if required by law

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure storage of API credentials, and access controls to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at Carrercurve@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
